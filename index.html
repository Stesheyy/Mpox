Mpox Awareness: Your Health, Our Priority – Learn, Prevent, and Protect Today!

At Mpox Awareness, we work with individuals, communities, and organizations to promote prevention, provide reliable information, and offer resources to help reduce the spread of Mpox. Through education and practical guidance, we aim to empower communities to make informed health choices, safeguard well-being, and support a healthier future for all.

Mpox (formerly known as Monkeypox) is a viral disease that primarily occurs in Central and West Africa, but has been spreading to other regions in recent years. It is similar to smallpox but less severe, causing symptoms like fever, rash, and swollen lymph nodes. Early detection and treatment are crucial in preventing complications. To learn more about how to protect yourself and others, click the links below to explore detailed information on Mpox symptoms, transmission, treatment options, prevention strategies, and available vaccines.
